# Launch copy

## Primary post

Menu Band turns the right side of your Mac's menu bar into an instrument.

Click the tiny keys, type while the app is focused, plug in a MIDI keyboard, or
send the notes into any DAW through its virtual MIDI source. Version 1.6.5 adds
hardware MIDI input and a separate CDJ Radio deck for KPBJ, R8DIO, NTS 1, and
NTS 2—with a scratchable live buffer and sample-to-piano capture.

$4.99 on the Mac App Store. Open source, by Aesthetic.Computer.

## Advanced-edition note

The Mac App Store edition is the clean sandboxed instrument. The separately
notarized Advanced edition adds host-level integrations that the sandbox cannot
support, including global typing, Spotify process audio, and room audio.

<https://menuband.app/advanced>
