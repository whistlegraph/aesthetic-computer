beat 6 — THE FIRST SHEEP. a single needle-felted SHEEP — wonderfully
woolly off-white body, little dark grey felt face and slim legs —
peeks in from the gallery doorway at frame edge, looking toward the
drowsy felt jeffrey doll on the bench in the middle distance, his
head fully drooped now. the vast felted water-lily tapestry behind.
the moment reads: the counting is about to begin.
