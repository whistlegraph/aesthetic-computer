beat 5 — HEAVY EYES. the felt jeffrey doll sits on the long backless
felt bench in front of the vast felted water-lily tapestry, slumping
sideways, eyelids drooping — felted eyes almost shut, head nodding
toward his shoulder. the tiny felted boba now stands on the bench
beside him, half-finished. his red felt glasses slipping down his
little wool nose. the pond painting glows softly behind.
