beat 8 — THE DREAM. inside the painting: the felt jeffrey doll floats
curled-up and asleep ON the felted water-lily pond itself — resting
on a giant soft wool lily pad among felted blossoms, blues and
greens and lilac wool rippling around him. above, two woolly sheep
