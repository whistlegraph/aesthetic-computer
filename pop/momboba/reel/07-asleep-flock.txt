beat 7 — ASLEEP AMONG THE FLOCK (the cover scene, wide and vertical).
the felt jeffrey doll lies fully asleep along the felt gallery bench,
one arm folded under his head, red felt glasses folded by his hand,
felted boba standing beside his head. scattered across the felt floor
around the bench, a small flock of needle-felted sheep — three or
four, some grazing, one turned toward him as if just counted. the
monumental felted water-lily tapestry fills the wall above. dim,
tender, hushed.
