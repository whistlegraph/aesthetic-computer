movement 4 — DEEP / THE POND ROOM. wide landscape: the felt jeffrey
doll stands tiny at the lower left before a MONUMENTAL felted
water-lily tapestry that fills the entire wide wall edge to edge —
soft impressionist blues, greens and lilac wool blended like
brushstrokes, a sleeping pond in wool. he gazes up in awe, boba
dangling loosely from one hand, head tilted back. a long backless
felt gallery bench waits in front of the tapestry, running across the
width. dim, reverent gallery light.
