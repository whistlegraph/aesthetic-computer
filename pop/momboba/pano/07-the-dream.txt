movement 7 — VIVID / THE DREAM (the climax, wide). inside the
painting: the felt jeffrey doll floats curled-up and asleep ON the
felted water-lily pond itself — resting on a giant soft wool lily pad
at centre among felted blossoms. seen from ABOVE AND BEHIND, his face
turned away from the camera and tucked down into the crook of his
felt arm — only the back and side of his tousled brown wool head and
his curled sleeping body read, NO front-facing face, no clear
portrait. blues and greens and lilac wool ripple outward across the
whole wide frame. above and to the sides, two or three woolly sheep
drift slowly across a felt sky like clouds. everything soft,
weightless, luminous, the richest most saturated wool of the whole
night — deeply asleep, the happiest dream.
