movement 3 — DESCEND / WANDERING THE GALLERIES. wide landscape: the
felt jeffrey doll strolls through a long felt gallery corridor that
recedes across the whole width of the frame, boba in hand, looking up
at framed ABSTRACT FELT artworks on the walls — soft color-field
rectangles in muted wool (rust, indigo, cream), each in a thin felt
frame, no text anywhere. his stride is slowing, posture starting to
soften, eyelids lowering. quiet top-light spilling down the long
empty corridor.
