movement 5 — DREAM / THE FIRST SHEEP. wide landscape: the felt
jeffrey doll sits slumped and drowsy on the long felt bench at centre
before the vast felted water-lily tapestry, head drooping, red felt
glasses slipping down his little wool nose, the half-finished felted
boba standing on the bench beside him. a single needle-felted SHEEP —
wonderfully woolly off-white body, little dark grey felt face and
slim legs — peeks in from the gallery doorway at the right frame
edge, looking toward him. the moment reads: the counting is about to
begin. dim, hushed.
