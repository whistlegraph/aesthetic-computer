movement 6 — STILL / ASLEEP AMONG THE FLOCK (the cover scene, wide).
the felt jeffrey doll lies fully asleep along the long felt gallery
bench at centre, one arm folded under his head, red felt glasses
folded by his hand, finished felted boba standing beside his head.
scattered across the wide felt floor around the bench, a small flock
of needle-felted sheep — four or five, some grazing, one turned
toward him as if just counted — spread out across the width of the
frame. the monumental felted water-lily tapestry fills the wall
above. dim, tender, deeply hushed.
