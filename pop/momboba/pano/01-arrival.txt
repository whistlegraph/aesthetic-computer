movement 1 — DUSK / ARRIVAL. wide landscape: the felt jeffrey doll
walks up the felt sidewalk toward a grand modern-art museum entrance
at the right of frame — a tall minimalist felt facade in warm greys
with big blank glass doors (felt-framed, no text, no banners), a few
felt street trees. the long sidewalk and a soft pale dusk-wool sky
fill the open left of the frame. he carries a tiny felted boba tea
(pale milk-tea cup, domed top, fat pastel straw, dark wool pearls at
the bottom) in one hand, mid-stride, hopeful. soft dimming daylight.
