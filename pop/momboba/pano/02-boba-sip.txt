movement 2 — DROWSE / THE SIP. wide landscape: the felt jeffrey doll
stands in a tall white felt museum lobby, taking a long happy sip
from his tiny felted boba — fat pastel straw to his mouth, eyes
half-lidded with satisfaction, little dark wool tapioca pearls
visible through the pale cup. he is set left-of-centre; the rest of
the wide atrium opens out to the right — one big soft hanging felt
mobile turning in the out-of-focus distance, a tall felt pillar.
hushed warm lobby light, lots of calm empty wool space.
