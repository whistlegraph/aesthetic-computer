movement 9 — RESOLVE / HOME. wide landscape: a final calm wide view
of the quiet gallery — the felt jeffrey doll sits up at one end of
the long felt bench, rested and softly smiling, the empty felted boba
cup cradled in his lap, his small felt shoulder bag back across his
body. the felt sheep have curled down to sleep themselves, dotted
peacefully across the wide felt floor. warm even daylight fills the
pond room; the monumental water-lily tapestry glows gently behind.
settled, content, home. the whole scene at rest.
