movement 8 — MORNING. wide landscape: the same long felt gallery
bench at centre, but now pale dawn light spills in from tall felt
windows along the left wall, warming the wool from grey to soft
peach. the felt jeffrey doll is just beginning to stir — still lying
down but lifting his tousled wool head a little, red felt glasses
back on, blinking awake. the empty felted boba cup lies tipped on its
side beside him, a few stray wool pearls spilled. one or two felt
sheep linger, settling. the water-lily tapestry glows softly in the
new light. tender, calm, waking.
