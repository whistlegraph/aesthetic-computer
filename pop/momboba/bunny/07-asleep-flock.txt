beat 7 — ASLEEP AMONG THE FLOCK (the hero scene, wide and vertical).
the felt bunny lies fully asleep along the felt gallery bench, both
long ears flopped flat and soft, one round paw folded under his
head, his eyes felted gently shut. his red felt glasses are OFF his
face — folded closed on the bench near his paw, clearly not worn.
the finished felted boba stands beside his head. scattered across the felt floor
around the bench, a small flock of needle-felted sheep — three or
four, woolly off-white with little dark grey faces, some grazing,
one turned toward him as if just counted. the monumental felted
water-lily tapestry fills the wall above. dim, tender, hushed.
