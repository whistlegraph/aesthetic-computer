beat 8 — THE DREAM. inside the painting: the felt bunny floats
curled-up and asleep ON the felted water-lily pond itself — resting
on a giant soft wool lily pad among felted blossoms, blues and
greens and lilac wool rippling around him, BOTH of his two long ears
spread soft and loose across the pad. he wears his round red felt
glasses — his two eyes felted gently shut, each closed eye centered
INSIDE its round red lens, the rims never empty. his tiny felted boba
tea (pale milk-tea cup, domed top, fat pastel straw, dark wool
tapioca pearls) drifts on the pond right beside him, floating on a
little lily pad of its own. above, two woolly sheep drift slowly
across a felt sky like clouds. everything soft, weightless, deeply
asleep. the happiest ending.
