beat 2 — THE SIP. close-up of the felt bunny in the museum lobby,
taking a long happy sip from his tiny felted boba — fat pastel straw
to his mouth, bead eyes half-lidded with satisfaction, one long ear
mid-twitch with delight, little dark wool tapioca pearls visible
through the pale cup. both paws cradle the cup. behind him,
out-of-focus felt lobby: a tall white felt atrium, one big soft
hanging felt mobile. hushed lobby light.
