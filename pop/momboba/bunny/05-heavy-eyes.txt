beat 5 — HEAVY EYES. the felt bunny sits on the long backless felt
bench in front of the vast felted water-lily tapestry, slumping
sideways, bead eyes drooping nearly shut, head nodding toward his
shoulder — and both long ears sagging softly halfway down, heavy
with sleep. his little red felt glasses slip down his pink nose. the
tiny felted boba now stands on the bench beside him, half-finished.
the pond painting glows softly behind.
