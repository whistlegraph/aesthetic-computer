beat 6 — THE FIRST SHEEP. a single needle-felted SHEEP — wonderfully
woolly off-white body, little dark grey felt face and slim legs
(clearly a sheep, short ears, nothing like the bunny) — peeks in
from the gallery doorway at frame edge, looking toward the drowsy
felt bunny on the bench in the middle distance, his head fully
drooped now, one long ear flopped all the way over. the vast felted
water-lily tapestry behind. the moment reads: the counting is about
to begin.
