beat 4 — THE POND ROOM. the felt bunny stands tiny before a
MONUMENTAL felted water-lily tapestry that fills the entire wall edge
to edge — soft impressionist blues, greens and lilac wool blended
like brushstrokes, a sleeping pond in wool. he gazes up in awe, head
tilted back, both long ears perked straight up one last time in
wonder, boba dangling loosely from one paw. a long backless felt
gallery bench waits behind him. dim, reverent gallery light.
