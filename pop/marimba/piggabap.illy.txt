piggabap — album cover for a fast cartoon-barnyard chase track.

a colored-pencil + gouache drawing on warm cream paper, square.
confident hatching and striping build tone — tapered pencil edges,
visible paper grain, optical mixing in the pink-and-pale-spotted
piglet hides. diegetic light only — clean midday sun overhead,
short shadows, no overlay shine, no glow filter. NOT a photograph,
NOT cinematic.

two figures lean over the top rail of a low wooden pen at equal
eye-line, both seen from about the chest up, neither centered as a
hero. heads in the upper half of the frame. neither looks at the
camera; both gaze down into the pen at the piglets, calm grins, a
touch of grown-up amusement. wide awake, daytime, alert.

on the left — jeffrey, about 30, tousled medium-length brown hair,
a thin red glasses-cord at his neck. he wears a light-blue long-
sleeve BUTTON-DOWN SHIRT with a faint vertical pinstripe and a real
patch chest pocket on the left side — the outfit from his reference
photos. NOT a sweater, no sweater anywhere on him. his yellow
Sailor Pro Gear fountain pen — short cigar-shaped barrel, flat-
topped cap, polished metal clip, gold trim ring — is CLIPPED INTO
that shirt chest pocket: the clip hooked over the pocket's top hem,
the barrel tucked down inside so only the capped top stands above
the pocket. drawn clean and accurate, never a ballpoint or marker.
one piglet has reared up onto its hind legs and is nibbling a fold
of jeffrey's button-down sleeve at the cuff — jeffrey is grinning
down at it, not pulling away.

on the right — a farmer peer, an adult about jeffrey's age, in
washed denim bib overalls over a soft chambray shirt, a wide-brim
straw hat tipped slightly back, neutral grin. one of their hands
rests easily on the wooden top rail of the pen. equal eye-line with
jeffrey, neither centered.

inside the pen below the rail: four or five pink-and-spotted
piglets in cheerful disorder — one nibbling jeffrey's cuff, two
nosing into a shallow mud puddle in the foreground, one curled
sleeping in clean straw, one snout-up looking back at the farmer.
hatched pinks and warm greys for the hides, small black hooves,
pricked ears.

beside the pen on a hay bale: a chartreuse MacBook Neo, LID-BACK to
camera — only the back of the lid faces us, keyboard plane out of
sight behind the hay. a torn little white paper scrap on the lid
hand-penned with a thick whistlegraph butterfly (NOT an apple logo,
NOT a pals logo, NOT any brand). no other devices, no other props.

palette: warm cream paper ground; pale-sky-blue overhead; sap green
pasture behind the pen; sun-bleached wood-brown rails; light-blue
pinstripe shirt, denim bib, chambray, straw-yellow hat; pink and
warm-grey spotted piglet hides; chocolate-brown mud with a wet
highlight; buttery yellow on jeffrey's pen; one coral pop on the
glasses-cord; chartreuse on the Neo. hand-drawn, hatched, print-
tech-aware. clean midday sunlight, no haze, no neon, no glow.

square album cover. no text, no wordmarks, no readable logos.
neither figure looks at the camera.
