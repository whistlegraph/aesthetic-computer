kookabap — album cover for a kookaburra-dawn pop track.

a colored-pencil + gouache drawing on warm cream paper, square.
confident hatching and striping build tone — tapered pencil edges,
visible paper grain, optical mixing in feather barbs and canopy
leaves. diegetic light only — golden raking sunrise light from
camera-right, dappling down through gum-tree leaves overhead.
NOT a photograph, NOT cinematic.

a head-and-shoulders crop of two figures standing side by side
under a tropical-park canopy at sunrise. their heads fill the
upper-middle frame — close, warm, album-cover close. peers at one
shared eye-line, neither centered as a hero. neither looks at the
camera; both gaze upward and to the right, calmly tracking the
sound of laughter coming from a branch above.

both are wide awake — alert, soft-smiling, daybreak attention.
eyes open, faces lifted. unhurried, present, dawn.

on the left — jeffrey, about 30, tousled medium-length brown hair,
a thin red glasses-cord at his neck. he wears a light-blue
long-sleeve BUTTON-DOWN SHIRT with a faint vertical pinstripe and
a real patch chest pocket on the left side. NOT a sweater, no
sweater anywhere on him. his yellow Sailor Pro Gear fountain pen —
short cigar-shaped barrel, flat-topped cap, polished metal clip,
gold trim ring — is CLIPPED INTO that shirt chest pocket: the clip
hooked over the pocket's top hem, the barrel tucked down inside so
only the capped top stands above the pocket. drawn clean and
accurate, never a ballpoint or marker.

on the right — an ornithologist peer about jeffrey's age, calm
neutral smile, gently focused upward. binoculars hang around the
neck on a worn canvas strap. a khaki short-sleeve field vest over
a plain off-white tee, the vest pockets squared and visible.
no hat, hair cropped close.

across the upper branch above both figures: three kookaburras
lined up — chunky kingfisher silhouettes, oversized heads, blunt
beaks slightly open as if mid-cackle. their feathers drawn
hand-painted, individual barbs visible — buff cream chest, dark
brown wing bars, a glint of turquoise blue on the wing edges. one
bird's head is tipped back laughing, the other two listening.

across the bottom-middle of the frame: a chartreuse MacBook Neo
open on a folded canvas camp stool between the two figures,
LID-BACK to camera — only the back of the lid faces us, the
keyboard plane completely out of frame below. a torn little white
paper scrap on the lid hand-penned with a thick whistlegraph
butterfly (NOT an apple logo, NOT a pals logo, NOT any brand).
no other devices, no other props.

palette: warm cream paper ground; soft sap-green and ochre tropical
canopy leaves; dappled golden raking sunrise from camera-right;
light-blue cotton shirt; khaki vest and off-white tee; warm brown
hair and warm grey; buttery yellow on jeffrey's pen, turquoise + buff
+ brown on the kookaburras; one coral pop on the glasses-cord;
chartreuse on the Neo. hand-drawn, hatched, print-tech-aware.
golden dawn light, no haze, no neon, no glow filters.

square album cover. no text, no wordmarks, no readable logos.
neither figure looks at the camera.
