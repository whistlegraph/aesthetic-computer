and gouache drawing on pure white paper — but a NOCTURNAL, smoky,
late-night version. warm hand-made grain, soft washes. NOT slick, NOT
digital, NOT 3-D. lowercase, moody, dreamy. NO text, NO words, NO
letters, NO logos, NO readable brand marks (the hand-drawn "5" on the
ball is the ONLY number).

this is the DUB mix — spacious, echoing, deep. the whistle is drenched in
echo, so the drawn whistle-line REPEATS in fading ghost-trails.

green-felt pool table, honey-wood rail gone shadowy, a dark leather

glow the only warm light in a cool blue-purple room.

flows from jeffrey's pursed lips and arcs the two 5-balls toward the
