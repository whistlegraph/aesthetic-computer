Dis-order

Dis-order is a performance, communal ritual, and a family drama that asks what forces move through us when we enact an ancient spring rite, and what is left unspoken when we gather around the table.

Staged a la Judy Chicago's The Dinner Party, the piece references the Passover seder, Greek mythology, and Pina Bausch's Rite of Spring to examine the performance of ritual and the rigidity of roles and stories within families, communities, and institutions.
