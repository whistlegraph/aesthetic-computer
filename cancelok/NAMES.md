## Pedal analogy (subagent, 2026-07-14)

Mining the GUITAR PEDAL / stompbox / pedalboard world. The unlock: each self-running
pad IS a stompbox (one glowing thing it does), and **wandering = walking a pedalboard**.
The deepest fit is the *linger-vs-flick* signal тЖТ in pedal terms, engaging a pedal vs
leaving it on **true bypass**. Stomp/pad also literally bridges the app's own unit word
("pads") with the pedal verb.

Full candidate pool (rationale each):
- **stomppad** тАФ the app's real noun ("pad") fused with the pedal verb; the pads ARE
  stompboxes. A true bridge word, not an outside metaphor.
- **truebypass** тАФ flick past = true bypass, linger = engaged. Deepest concept fit;
  slightly technical/long.
- **fuzzgrid** тАФ "fuzz" is both a pedal type AND the glow/grain of the visuals; grid = the lattice.
- **stompgrid** тАФ clearest literal read: a grid of stompboxes you wander.
- **stompyard** тАФ a sprawling yard of pedals to roam; roomy, gig-poster energy.
- **stompweb** тАФ a web of stompboxes; echoes "web of pad-rooms" directly.
- **rigroam** тАФ roaming a guitar rig; alliterative, motion baked in.
- **toneroam** тАФ drifting through tones; soft, ambient.
- **patchbay** тАФ real audio term: a grid you route through тАФ literal metaphor.
- **bypass** тАФ single-word version of the linger/flick idea; generic, .com dead.
- **patchgrid** тАФ grid of pedal patches; a bit flat.
- **pedalpad** тАФ pedal + pad; cute but redundant and a tongue-twister.
- **tapgrid** тАФ tap-tempo + grid; you tap through it.
- **stomproom** тАФ the pad-rooms as stomp-rooms; fine, less punchy.
- **glowbox** тАФ glowing pad-room as a stompbox. Great fit but TAKEN (.app + .art).
- **rigwander / drivepad** тАФ motion/overdrive riffs; weaker distinctiveness.

### Ranked shortlist
1. **stomppad** тАФ top pick. Reuses the app's own word (pads) welded to the pedal verb;
   self-explains, no acronym to teach. `.app` + `.art` both free.
2. **truebypass** тАФ best pure-concept fit (linger=engaged / flick=bypass) but reads
   technical and long for a mascot-name. Both free.
3. **fuzzgrid** тАФ names the glow AND the pedal in five letters + grid. Both free.
4. **stompgrid** тАФ safest literal read; a touch generic. Both free.
5. **stompyard** тАФ evocative, roomy; "yard" drifts a little from "grid." Both free.
6. **rigroam** тАФ motion built in, alliterative; "rig" is less distinctive. Both free.

### Recommendation: **stomppad**
It beats the current leaders because it's the only candidate that fuses the app's OWN
vocabulary with the analogy тАФ "pad" is already what these units are called; "stomp"
just tells you they're pedals you step on. That makes it self-explaining where **ganny**
is an opaque acronym you have to be told (plus the faint "granny"). It carries none of
the mental-illness stigma of **schizholes**, and it has real texture and a built-in
gesture where **piece.place** is a generic domain-hack. Short, lowercase, one warm
object, and `stomppad.app` / `stomppad.art` are both free at standard price. If a more
conceptual name is wanted, **truebypass** is the runner-up тАФ it encodes the core
interaction (linger vs. flick) exactly.
