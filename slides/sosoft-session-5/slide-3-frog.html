A
the notepat charts deck
the 11-card learning deck already exists — layout, intervals, major / minor / dim / aug triads, 7ths, circle of fifths, scales, drills, progressions. phone-proportioned, pastel-tinted. teaches the instrument the room is about to play.
+ already designed · serves notepat · − 64 × 11 cards = real print cost
B
the keymap single sheet
single 5×7 print of the diagram you saw last meeting. cheapest, fastest, already designed. ships from any print shop in a week. backup or addendum to the deck.
+ cheapest answer · − says less than the deck
C
the usb stick
64 thumbdrives, each carrying AC Native OS + notepat preloaded. plug into any laptop, power on, hold a key. the most ambitious — also the most demonstrative.
+ the cohort takes home a bootable OS · − ~$120 in sticks + 1 day of imaging
D
the pixsies insert
printed sleeve for a pop/ single, qr to notepat.com on the back, download code inside. ties the music lane to the social-software conversation.
+ a record people can keep · − drifts toward "release" register
