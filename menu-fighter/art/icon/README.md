# App icon

`menu-fighter-icon-master.png` is the original 1254 px unmasked generation and
`menu-fighter-icon-1024.png` is its exact 1024 px app-icon export. They were
generated with GPT Image 2 from the canonical Pals logo and cross-counter study.
The art is intentionally free of text, platform badges, borders, and baked
rounded corners. Derive macOS icon sizes, web favicons/PWA assets, and Xbox
Store/tile art without overwriting the source.

Final prompt:

> Create a polished square master app-icon illustration for a cross-platform
> fighting game called Menu Fighter. Use the canonical Pals logo and the
> cross-counter concept as strict references. Preserve two friendly abstract
> tubular ribbon figures in warm carved wood, with loop heads and zigzag bodies.
> Pose them as a balanced mirrored cross-counter with forearms forming a clean
> central X. Simplify for recognition at 32 px, keep detail within the middle
> 70% safe area, and use a pale muted sage background with restrained studio
> dimensionality. No text, border, outer icon container, UI, weapons, faces,
> extra limbs, or scene.

Before release, redraw the selected silhouette as vector geometry, confirm the
two bodies are unambiguous at small sizes, and test platform masks plus grayscale
and high-contrast variants.
