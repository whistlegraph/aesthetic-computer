# Pals fighter-mark concepts

These four GPT Image 2 studies begin with the canonical wooden Pals mark and ask
how it can read as two fighters without losing its friendliness.

| File | Read | Best use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `pals-face-off.png` | two separate Pals at neutral | versus screen, matchmaking |
| `pals-fist-bump.png` | connection and sportsmanship | community, rematch, team identity |
| `pals-cross-counter.png` | compact competitive X | strongest icon silhouette |
| `pals-guard-strike.png` | asymmetric action | splash/editorial illustration |

The cross-counter was selected for the first icon master. These are concept
illustrations, not final vector trademarks; the eventual production mark should
be redrawn as clean geometry and tested at 16, 32, 64, and 1024 pixels.

## Prompt set

All four prompts referenced `xbox/assets/pals.png` and a Pals pose study. Shared
direction: preserve the canonical loop heads, continuous tubular ribbon limbs,
warm carved wood, and pale sage field; show exactly two abstract figures; no
text, faces, weapons, clothing, arena, border, UI, or unrelated objects.

- **Face-off:** Separate the mark into two mirrored Pals in balanced neutral
  fighting stances, facing one another across a small central gap.
- **Fist bump:** Make two mirrored Pals meet at the center in a compact fist
  bump, communicating rivalry, friendship, and rematch energy.
- **Cross-counter:** Make the two Pals throw simultaneous straight punches so
  their arms cross in a clean central X; keep the silhouette symmetric.
- **Guard and strike:** Put the left Pal in a compact guard and the right Pal in
  a long straight punch; favor readable action and editorial composition.
