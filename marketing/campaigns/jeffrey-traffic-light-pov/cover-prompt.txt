palette: warm cream paper as the ground. sun-bleached asphalt grey.
warm late-afternoon la gold. dusty olive on palm trunks. muted brick
and stucco pink for storefront walls. faded red and amber on signage
and awnings. one chartreuse-citrus pop on the laptop. cobalt blue on
the trouser hems. matte near-black on sneakers and laptop keys.
weathered slate on the signal cabinet and signal heads. hand-drawn,
hatched, print-tech-aware, print-comic restraint.

avoid: photoreal automotive or architectural rendering. cinematic
golden-hour glow as a css overlay — the warmth comes from diegetic
sun hatched into the marks. any visible laptop or phone screen
content. my face. any text labeling apps or signs that names a real
brand outright. cartoon exaggeration. fish-eye distortion. keep the
perspective believable — someone calmly cross-legged on top of a
signal cabinet, working on a laptop, looking down past it at la.
