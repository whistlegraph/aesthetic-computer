# jeffrey-fixing-andreessen-igstory

instagram story — vertical 9:16. companion piece to
`jeffrey-helping-elon-igstory`. same colored-pencil-on-cream tone,
same calm-craftsman jeffrey in service, peer-horizontality, NEVER
hero-pose.

## the joke

a sand-hill-road vc boardroom. marc andreessen is at the head of
the table — but he's actually a humanoid android, and the service
panel on the top of his head is hinged open. jeffrey, calmly typing
on his thinkpad, is ethernetted into the open panel via a yellow
cat6 patch. andreessen is frozen mid-word, eyes glassy on a slow
android blink-timer. the rest of the table — three other vc-types,
a term sheet, a pen poised over the signature line — waits politely
for jeffrey to finish so they can hear andreessen speak again and
get the paper signed. it's the calmest android service call ever
held in a boardroom.

## moderation guardrails

- never gore / never biological. the open panel is a CLEAN product-
  design interior: neatly bundled ribbon cables, small green pcb
  modules, labeled connector blocks, three diagnostic leds. exactly
  like opening the back of a laptop.
- no blood, no scalp, no skull, no flesh. "service panel" reads like
  a roomba's bottom or a robot vacuum's lid, not surgery.
- everyone fully clothed, business casual, calm. no panic, no harm,
  no menace. routine maintenance.

## composition

- jeffrey: same outfit as the elon campaign (light-blue pinstripe
  shirt + yellow bear emblem + yellow pen clip + red glasses on cord
  + cobalt linen trousers + black canvas low-tops).
- thinkpad on the table, lid AWAY from camera (no visible screen).
- inline diagnostic box with the green/amber/red leds on the table.
- yellow cat6 from laptop → diagnostic box → into the open panel on
  andreessen's head. both jeffrey's hands on the keyboard.
- term sheet + pen in the middle-foreground, signature line marked
  with an "X", poised but un-signed.
- 2–3 other people around the table at the same eye-line as jeffrey
  and andreessen — peer-horizontality. polite waiting, NOT panic.
