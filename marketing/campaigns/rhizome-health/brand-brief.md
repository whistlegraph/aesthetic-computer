# Rhizome Health — brand brief

A satirical AC marketing campaign: **Rhizome Health**, a fictional
health-insurance arm of [Rhizome.org](https://rhizome.org), provides
real health coverage to every Aesthetic Computer user who has
registered a `@handle` — the "handled" users.

The joke is the gig-economy / freelance-artist healthcare gap closed by
the unlikeliest provider: a small net-art nonprofit functioning as an
HMO. To get covered, you just need a handle.

## Concept — the net-art clinic exam room

An intimate, cheerful doctor's-office exam room, AC-flavored. A net
artist sits for a wellness checkup; the "insurance" is processed live —
a peer **intake clinician** types the patient's `@handle` into an AC
laptop and an enrollment goes through. jeffrey is the **patient**, a
peer in the frame — never the host, never centered.

Two figures, same eye-line. Colored-pencil + gouache on cream paper —
the AC house style for satirical tableaux.

## Rhizome — real brand identity (grounding)

Pulled from the live rhizome.org identity (2026):

- **Mark:** bright lime-green radiating lines fanning from a bottom-left
  corner on black — a rhizomatic root-spray. Exact green `#1FD61F`.
  (Source: the real rhizome.org favicon — `refs/rhizome-mark-favicon.png`.)
- **Wordmark:** "Rhizome" set in a clean Helvetica-like grotesk
  (the site ships **U001**, a URW grotesk). Caps or title-case.
- **Palette:** black ground, lime-green `#1FD61F`, an electric cyan
  accent `#64ffff`, warm grey `#c1c0c0`.
- **Voice:** "the international hub for new media art since 1996" —
  delivered as a plain header-sentence.

### Rhizome Health sub-brand lockup

`refs/rhizome-health-logo.png` — built here from the real identity:
the green radiating-roots mark + "**Rhizome** Health" wordmark (white +
green), a cyan co-brand line *"for Aesthetic Computer"*, and a grey
tagline *"handled users covered since 2028"*, all on black. This is the
logo that appears on clinic signage, posters, clipboards, and the
handle-cards in the scene — a Rhizome × Aesthetic Computer partnership.

## Scene cues

- **Handle-card** = the insurance card. A small laminated card,
  hand-lettered `@jeffrey`, the green rhizome root-mark in one corner.
- **Eye chart** = a kidlisp color test — descending rows of chunky
  multi-color hand-drawn shapes and short words.
- **AC laptop** on a rolling cart — the clinician types the handle; the
  screen shows a simple enrollment form, never a thumbnail of the room.
- jeffrey's chartreuse butterfly Neo sits closed on a side chair (his
  alone — the clinic laptop is a plain machine).
- Diegetic light only — daylight from a window, a warm desk lamp.

## Key rules carried in

- Peer horizontality — jeffrey off-center, same eye-line as the
  clinician, never the hero.
- Colored-pencil + perceptual breakdowns; describe the look by its
  qualities — no living-artist names with figures in frame.
- No recursive screens; jeffrey real outfit; USB-stick vape as a
  varying prop; diegetic light only.
- Wellness-checkup framing — no needles, syringes, injections, or blood
  (a moderation-safe surface).
