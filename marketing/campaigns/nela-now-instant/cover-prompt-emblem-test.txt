Hand-rendered emblem on warm cream-colored paper — designed to read like a
record-label seal or a hand-painted shop sign. ONE singular subject only:
a single colored-pencil-and-gouache drawing of a small art-house cinema
screen glowing flat deep emerald green (the color of British racing
green), a single short line of plain monospace text shown on it, with a
small folding table and a closed laptop on the table directly below the
screen. The emblem fills the central two-thirds of the square and
floats; corners and periphery DISSOLVE INTO PURE CREAM PAPER on all four
sides with NO hard frame, NO border, NO background scene. Drawn in
colored pencil and gouache with thick confident strokes, hatching for
tone, tapered pencil edges, visible paper grain. Muted natural palette:
warm cream paper, ivory whites, soft chocolate browns, brick red, lamp-
amber, deep emerald green. Square 1:1 composition. Hockney print-work
sensibility.
