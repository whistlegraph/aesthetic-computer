# NELA Computer Club × Now Instant Image Hall — brand brief

Two real Chinatown LA institutions, **literally next door** to each other:

- **Plot** — 937 Sun Mun Way (NELA Computer Club home base)
- **Now Instant Image Hall** — 939 Chung King Rd

## NELA Computer Club (https://nelacomputer.club)

- **Look:** pure black-on-white, monospace, ASCII / Unicode box-drawing logo:
  ```
  ▛▚   ▌   ▞▔▔▚   ▞▔▔▚
  ▌ ▚  ▌  ▞      ▞
  ▌  ▚ ▌  ▚      ▚
  ▌   ▚▌   ▚▂▂▞   ▚▂▂▞
  ```
  Heavy use of `░░░░░` / `▒▒▒▒▒` rules between sections.
- **Voice:** anti-establishment / leftist. *"All computing is political."* *"No god, no master."* PGA hallmarks + Recurse Center social rules.
- **Format:** every other Tuesday, 7pm, "bring a laptop." Creative computing meetup — alone-around-others or collaborative.
- **NELA** = Northeast LA region; meetup is in Chinatown (Plot).

## Now Instant Image Hall (https://now-instant.la)

- **Look:** body is `bg-black text-white`. Single accent color `text-green` = `rgb(0, 122, 51)` = **#007A33** (deep British-racing-green / emerald). Big uppercase sans-serif display type. Tabular-nums dates (`05.06.26`).
- **Wordmark:** "**Now Instant** ▭▭▭▭▭▭ **Image Hall**" — a colored rectangle / rule between the two halves. (The `logo-glyph` span is a visual block.)
- **Tagline:** "Now Instant Image Hall is a cinema and bookstore."
- **Programming:** experimental / underground cinema — Agnès Varda, Neil Young's *Human Highway*, William Pope.L videos, Bresson's *The Devil, Probably*, *Slow Machine*. Curatorial, avant-garde, art-world.
- **Venue:** small Chinatown screening room + bookstore. Signage hand-set, marquee minimal.

## Concept — "Big Screen Computer Club"

A one-night collab: NELA Computer Club takes over Now Instant for a **demo night**.
Members buy tickets, line up at the box office, and one by one plug into the cinema
projector to demo their thing on the big screen — a tiny program we put on. Picture
PechaKucha + a Chinatown art-house cinema + a hacker meetup. Jeffrey is **a peer
member** demoing alongside everyone else, never the host or center.

### Bridges between the two brands

- The marquee outside reads **NOW INSTANT** with a small pasted-on slip:
  *"TONIGHT — NELA COMPUTER CLUB"* (ASCII rules `░░░` framing it).
- Tickets are mimeographed black-on-white in NELA's monospace, with a single
  green Now Instant stamp.
- The screen glow is **#007A33** green (a kidlisp / terminal demo) — diegetic
  light source per our recap-photo rule.
- Bookstore shelves visible at the side.
