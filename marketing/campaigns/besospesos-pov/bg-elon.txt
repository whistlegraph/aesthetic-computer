a photograph of a colored-pencil and gouache drawing on warm cream
paper, tall vertical portrait. confident hatching and striping build
tone. tapered pencil edges, visible paper grain, optical mixing,
print-work sensibility. diegetic light only — no overlay glow, no
motion blur, no soft-focus haze. crisp marks foreground to back. the
drawing fills the whole frame edge to edge — no cream border, no
vignette.

first-person pov — this is what i see standing on the porch of a
sleek modern house at dusk. a wide matte-black front door, centered,
with a small keypad beside it glowing soft amber. through the tall
side window next to the door: a fine indoor mist — the sprinklers
are on inside the house — catching warm interior light in thin
hatched streaks. a porch lamp overhead throws a warm pool of light
on the concrete step.

the CENTER of the frame, in front of the door, is an empty welcome
mat on the step — calm and clear, nobody standing on it. that space
stays open.

at the very bottom edge of the frame: my own hand resting on the
dark steel porch rail.

no people anywhere in the scene. no readable text, no brand marks,
no screens.
