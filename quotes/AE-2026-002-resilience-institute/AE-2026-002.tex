% Each page lays out: header (fixed \headgap) -> modules joined by \modgap

\cathead{riteal}{How it should feel}
Calm, classical, a little alchemical. Warm off-white paper and deep ink, four
quiet section colors, a touch of gold. A classic serif for headings and a plain
typeface for reading --- nothing rounded or cute. Old anatomical and star-chart
engravings (the Robert Fludd drawing) carry the artwork; no stock photos of
candles, crystals, or yoga. The writing stays calm and precise --- it never
sounds like it is selling.
