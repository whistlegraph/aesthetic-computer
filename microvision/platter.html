Thesis Evolution

Phase 1: Pico Projectors (2015–2017)
Bet: MVIS laser beam scanning gets embedded in smartphones and robots.
Partners tracked: Sony, Sharp, Foxconn, Celluon, Ford.
Key product: Sony MPCL1 pico projector.
Phase 2: Interactive Displays & HoloLens (2018–2019)
Walt's conviction: Microsoft and Amazon are MVIS's "mystery customers."

Phase 3: Amazon Breadcrumbs (2020)

Apple/STM connection explored.

Jeffrey hosted files on aesthetic.computer for the Reddit/blog community.
Walt tracked Jeffrey's brokerage account, urged rebuy at $8.62.

Anduril (defense), ZF (auto Tier 1), NVIDIA (AI) partnerships.
Grok AI valuation: $2–5B market cap, $200–500M NED licensing.
