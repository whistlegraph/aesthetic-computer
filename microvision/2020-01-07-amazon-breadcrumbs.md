# Amazon Breadcrumbs Investigation

**Date:** 2020-01-07
**From:** Walt Scudder → Jeffrey Alan Scudder
**Subject:** Fwd: MVIS - Amazon crumbs / Peters blog about MVIS/AMZN

Walt's detective work connecting MicroVision to Amazon, shared with Peter (MVIS blogger) and then forwarded to Jeffrey.

## Walt's Discovery

1. MVIS published a new product data sheet for their interactive display module
2. The projected image showed a "poached salmon platter" for $129.99
3. The description was **identical** to a Whole Foods Bellevue, WA listing
4. The projector housing on the data sheet resembled the Amazon Echo speaker housing

Walt's conclusion: MVIS is building an interactive display for a new Amazon Echo device using their Class 1 laser technology.

> "There are now millions of Amazon customers who own and are very familiar using the current cylindrical shaped Echo device. The thought of adding a similar looking device that offers new interactive display features... makes for an immediate captive audience."

## Peter's Response

Peter (petersmvis.blogspot.com) confirmed:

> "I'm already virtually certain that there is a relationship. More than a year ago I spoke to an Amazon engineer who worked with Alexa, and her reaction on being asked 'when is the interactive laser projector going to be included' was **shock and startle** enough to confirm all the other rumors I had encountered."

> "I don't think it's random either. I'll post this, because it's cool."
