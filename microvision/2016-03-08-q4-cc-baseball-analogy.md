# Q4 Conference Call: "Bottom of the First Inning"

**Date:** 2016-03-08
**From:** Walt Scudder → Jeffrey Alan Scudder
**Subject:** MVIS - FYI

Walt's detailed analysis after the Q4 2015 conference call.

## Baseball Analogy

> "I sorta look at this like a baseball game — it's the bottom of the first inning and MVIS has a runner (Sharp) on first and another runner (Sony) on second with no outs."

## Key Developments Tracked
- **Sharp Robohon** — robot/phone with MVIS laser projector, launching Q2-3 2016
- **Sony Xperia Agent** — smart device with MVIS laser projector
- **Foxconn buying Sharp** — implications for scale manufacturing
- **Sony building new smartphone factory** — first in 20 years
- **Short interest at ~18%** — Walt believed this suppressed the post-CC price

## Walt's Thesis

> "Sony, Sharp and Foxconn are major players in these markets — and the fact that they are building these plants and also incorporating MVIS laser projector technology into their products tells me that they foresee a big demand for these types of new products in the next decade."
