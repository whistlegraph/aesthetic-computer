# CES 2016: Sony MPCL1 + Ford HUD

**Date:** 2016-01-06
**From:** Walt Scudder → Roger Collamati
**Subject:** MVIS - CES

Walt's CES preview. He and Tricia gave Jeffrey a Sony MPCL1 pico projector (using MicroVision engine) for Christmas 2015.

## Products at CES Using MVIS Tech
- **Sony MPCL1** — pico projector prominently displayed in Sony's exhibit
- **Celluon PicoPro & PicoAir** — smartphone-sized portable projectors ($299–$450)
- **Ford HUD** — heads-up display partnership with MicroVision

> "Don't know if MVIS will get any wide coverage at CES in next few days — I still think they are bound by non disclosure agreements from revealing who they are working with."

## Significance
MVIS tech was present at CES through partners (Sony, Celluon, Ford) even though MicroVision itself didn't have a prominent booth — the "embedded everywhere, named nowhere" pattern that would define the next decade.
