## Format if accepted

- 800–1500 words
- Engaging, conversational, sometimes experiential tone
- Opens with a "hook"
- Targets beginning art history students
- Object-based: focused on a **single work of art or monument**; biography and
  context flow from the object
- Includes visual analysis that walks the reader through the work
- Includes definitions for specialized terms (assume curious reader knows
  nothing)
- Resources list at the bottom; first link is the institution where the object
  lives
