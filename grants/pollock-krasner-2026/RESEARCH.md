# Pollock-Krasner Foundation Artist Grant — Research

> **Status:** Rolling; application preparation started 2026-07-20
> **Apply:** https://bbgm-apply.yourcausegrants.com/apply/programs/afa38443-4f9b-41de-9d80-b14f36899241
> **Guidelines:** https://www.pkf.org/how-to-apply/
> **Award:** Up to $50,000 unrestricted; PKF says most grants are about $35,000
> **Review:** Approximately 9–12 months

## Eligibility gate

- Professional visual artist working **primarily** in painting, sculpture, or works on paper (including printmaking).
- Active professional gallery/museum exhibition practice.
- At least 10 exhibitions during the past 10 years at professional galleries, museums, or recognized exhibition spaces.
- Submitted work must have been completed within the past 10 years.
- Students are ineligible (PhD candidates are not treated as students).
- Primarily photography, video/film, performance, graphic/commercial art, illustration, craft, generative art, or AI-generated art is ineligible.

The application should therefore lead with Jeffrey's physical painting, sculpture, and works-on-paper practice. Aesthetic Computer can appear as professional context, but digital/generative work must not be represented as the primary submitted practice.

## Required materials

1. Online application form.
2. One-page cover letter: purpose, requested amount, and how funds support the artistic career.
3. Current résumé/CV with solo and group exhibitions separated and listed newest first.
4. One-page artist statement specifically describing the ten submitted images.
5. Image identification list in exact image order: artist, image number, year, title, dimensions, medium.
6. Ten recent JPEGs.
7. Three professional references (contact details only unless PKF later requests letters).

Written attachments must be `.doc`, `.docx`, or `.odt`, in English, with the professional name on each document and in each filename. Images must be JPEG, exactly 2100 px on the longest edge, 72 or 96 DPI, highest quality, and named `Scudder_Jeffrey_1.jpg` through `Scudder_Jeffrey_10.jpg`. At least eight images must show separate individual works; no more than two may be details, alternate views, or installation views.

## Important process constraints

- There is no submission deadline.
- An online draft is automatically deleted after 120 days of inactivity.
- Only one application may be submitted in a 24-month period; withdrawal or deletion after submission does not reset this limit.
- Submitted applications cannot be revised.
- Do not upload videos, catalogues, a detailed budget, or unrequested materials.

## Before opening the portal

- Confirm the ten-exhibition eligibility threshold from the current CV.
- Select a coherent body of ten eligible physical works.
- Choose three references and confirm their current contact information.
- Decide the request amount and one-year use-of-funds story.
- Finish upload-ready documents and JPEGs before creating a portal draft, so the 120-day inactivity clock is not wasted.
