% ============ PROFILE ============
\section*{Profile}
Artist, educator, and toolmaker who builds creative-computing instruments for
other people to use and own. Creator of \textbf{Aesthetic Computer} --- a
runtime, bare-metal operating system, and online commons for creative computing
(\textasciitilde2,900 registered members; 400+ community-authored programs;
20,000+ public commits) --- and of \textbf{Whistlegraph}, \textbf{KidLisp}, and
\textbf{No Paint}. Work centers on re-founding the personal computer as a public,
commonly-owned thing, run on salvaged hardware and legible all the way down.
