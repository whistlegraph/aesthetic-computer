# Restless Egg — concept film · BRAND BRIEF

source-of-truth concept for the stills deck. one cohesive painterly look,
jeffrey **recognizable and front-lit** (not a silhouette — this film is a
person making a case, and the person is the point). informed by real AC lore
and real AC UI, in the marketing/`gen-promo` lineage.

> supersedes the silhouette/3-4-back rule in `NARRATIVE.md`. that rule existed
> only to survive Seedance face-moderation; we dropped Seedance, so jeffrey's
> face comes back.

## who he is (identity, locked from refs)

a painter — Ringling, then Yale School of Art — who spent the last decade
deciding the computer is paint. late thirties, warm brown eyes, brown stubble,
medium brown hair. grounded by `marketing/lib/jeffrey-refs.mjs` (SHOOT +
SELFIE). he must read as clearly **him** in every frame.

## the pitch (what the film argues)

aesthetic inc. is an instruments studio. notepat is the flagship. the commons
stays open; the company productizes instruments you *play* — not tools that
make you efficient. restless egg funds the studio.

## the look (cohesive across all 4 stills)

painterly digital illustration, hand-painted indie-animated-film concept-art
feel — warm, moody, rich color, soft glow, gentle film grain. cinematic 16:9.
aesthetic computer palette: warm cream, deep violet, citrus-green terminal
glow, coral accents. no readable text, no logos, no wordmarks.

## the four beats (painter → instrument → commons → invitation)

each beat is **visually distinct** (studio → machine-bloom → galaxy → stage) —
this fixes the old "every panel is the same laptop" problem. jeffrey is
recognizable in all four.

1. **the painter** — a cluttered LA studio at night, green-terminal glow + warm
   desk-lamp. jeffrey at the desk, **3/4-front, face catching the lamp light**,
   a painter's posture, brush in one hand — but the "canvas" is a laptop screen
   full of glowing light. a quiet stack of dead, shut laptops beside him (the
   e-waste). *VO: "I'm a painter who spent the last decade building instruments
   instead of paintings."*

2. **the awakening** — close on jeffrey, **face lit warm by the screen-bloom**,
   as his hands flip open a salvaged ThinkPad and slot a USB stick. the screen
   blooms a vivid grid of **real notepat note-tiles** + a bright waveform (use
   the captured `ac-native-notepat` ref so the screen is the real instrument).
   light floods out of the machine onto his face. *VO: "It boots a salvaged
   laptop straight into an instrument — no operating system underneath it."*

3. **the commons** — pull way back: one glowing screen becomes thousands, a dark
   constellation of little screens each blinking on with a distinct KidLisp
   program, threads of light connecting them. jeffrey in the lower foreground,
   **3/4 turned so we still catch his face**, looking up at the galaxy of
   makers. *VO: "It grows out of Aesthetic Computer — where eighteen thousand
   people have made sixteen thousand little programs."*

4. **the invitation** — one refurbished laptop on a warm, softly-spotlit dark
   stage, glowing like an instrument waiting to be played. jeffrey has just set
   it down and turns **toward us, face in the rim light**, a hopeful half-smile —
   an offer, not a retreat. *VO: "I want to make computing something you play
   again. That's what I'd do with Restless Egg."*

## motion

no generative video. each still gets a slow **Ken Burns** push/pan (ffmpeg
zoompan), timed to its VO line, hard cuts between beats. bed = notepat playing
itself under the trimmed jeffrey-pvc VO. ~25–30s.
