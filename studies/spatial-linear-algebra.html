A 2×2 matrix is a recipe for moving space. The columns say where the basis vectors land: î → (a, c), ĵ → (b, d). The image lives on the unit square; every pixel rides the same transform. Determinant = how much area is stretched (negative = the space flips over). Edit the matrix, or solve the problems.
