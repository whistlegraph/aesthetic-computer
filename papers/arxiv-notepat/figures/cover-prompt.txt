Saturated primary-colored cap (a confident chromatic hue), warm ivory piano-key flank, slate-grey cast shadow beneath. The single letter on the cap doubles as a musical note-name. Chunky, tactile, silkscreen-imperfect edges, high tonal contrast at the center softening to nothing at the edges. Bold and recognizable from across a room — a keycap you could play a scale on.
