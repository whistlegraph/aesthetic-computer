A single floating arched cathedral-window facade — one tall 19th-century red-brick industrial arched window rendered as a sculptural object hovering centered in the frame. Inside the arch, faintly visible, layered glass-collage shards (signature Yellin-style sculpture) are suspended in a vertical column, each shard containing a tiny abstract figure. Cast-iron column outlines flank the arch faintly. The arch is densely drawn with confident pencil strokes; the surrounding cream paper holds the form. Edges and corners fade fully into nothing.
