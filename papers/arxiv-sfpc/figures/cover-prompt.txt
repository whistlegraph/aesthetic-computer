A single small black classroom chalkboard floating dead-centre on cream paper, framed in worn pine. Drawn across the board in white chalk, slightly smudged: a loose hand-sketched LED-and-resistor schematic — a battery symbol, a resistor zigzag, an LED triangle with two emission lines, a closed wire loop. No lettering, no school name, no chalk words anywhere — only the schematic's symbols and a small flourish of chalk-dust gesture. A short dusty piece of chalk rests on the lower frame ledge. The chalkboard floats with no wall, no easel, no surface, no shadow.
