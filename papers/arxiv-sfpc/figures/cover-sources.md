# Cover-prompt sources — School for Poetic Computation

The cover is a **single floating book opened to a 90-degree spread**, half circuit-board, half handwritten cursive, with a breadboard + LED below. Each element traces to a real reference.

## Visual elements → sources

| Element | Source |
|---|---|
| **The half-circuit-board, half-handwritten book** | The SFPC posture made visual. Phrase: *"To Radical Openness and Generosity"* — the title of the school's public github finance repo and the founding mission. Co-founders Taeyoon Choi (Korean-American artist), Zach Lieberman (openFrameworks), Jen Lowe, and Amit Pitaru framed the school as a *poetic* engagement with code. The book = the curriculum; circuit + handwriting = the synthesis. Source: [sfpc.io/faq](https://sfpc.io/faq), [github.com/SFPC/finance-and-administration](https://github.com/SFPC/finance-and-administration). |
| **The breadboard + LED beneath the book** | The school's signature workshop object. SFPC's hardware-and-electronics curriculum strand (e.g., *Unconventional Computing*, *Code Societies*) puts breadboards in front of every student. Photos: SFPC's own session-archive pages at sfpc.io/[session-tag]. |
| **Glowing strands crossing between circuit and handwriting at the spine** | Visualizes "code as poetry" — the school's defining slogan. SFPC's instructional materials repeatedly cross-pollinate between technical and poetic registers; the strands are the metaphor made visible. |
| **Cream-paper edges fading to nothing** | Vignette; deliberately omits the **155 Bank Street, Westbeth** courtyard surroundings to keep the cover iconic-not-narrative. |

## Founder + key people referenced

The cover's "single book" is meant as a stand-in for the four co-founders' joint project, not any one of them:
- **Taeyoon Choi** — co-founder, ongoing director-figure through ~2020. [Personal site](https://taeyoonchoi.com).
- **Zach Lieberman** — co-founder; originator of openFrameworks; LLC partner until publicly stepping down in 2020. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zach_Lieberman).
- **Jen Lowe** — co-founder, teacher.
- **Amit Pitaru** — co-founder, teacher.
- Current Co-Directors **Todd Anderson + Neta Bomani** (2024–) take the school forward in the post-2020 governance structure but are not visually depicted.

## What was deliberately excluded

- The cohort-around-a-table scene (used in the original prompt iteration; rewritten as a single-form vignette).
- Specific student or faculty likenesses (would single out individuals; the school is collective).
- Westbeth Artists Housing facade (Westbeth is a building, not the school).
