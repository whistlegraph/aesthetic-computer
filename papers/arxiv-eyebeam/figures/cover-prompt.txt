A single floating eye-shaped form, drawn as a sculptural object. The iris is rendered as concentric rings of circuit-board pathways radiating outward like a printed-circuit eye. The pupil is solid and dark. Subtle filaments and fiber-optic-like strands extend from the eye's outer edges and dissolve into the cream paper. The form has the presence of a beacon or insignia — bold, simple, recognizable from a distance. Centered composition, densest at the pupil, fading to nothing at the corners.
