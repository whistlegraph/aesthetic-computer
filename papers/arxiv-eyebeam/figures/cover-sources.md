# Cover-prompt sources — Eyebeam

The cover is a **single floating eye-shaped form** with concentric circuit-board pathways radiating outward as the iris, a solid pupil at center, fiber-optic filaments at the edges. Each element is sourced.

## Visual elements → sources

| Element | Source |
|---|---|
| **Eye-shaped form** | Direct visual pun on the org's name — "Eyebeam." This is the most-used metaphor in the org's own communications and merchandise. The eye = the gaze of the artist on technology, and technology's gaze back. |
| **Concentric circuit-board pathways as the iris** | Eyebeam's founding identity: "art and technology" residency. From founding (1996–98 by John S. Johnson III) through current programs, the residency has paired artists with technical collaborators. The circuit-iris collapses both halves into one image. |
| **Solid pupil at center** | A focal anchor; the cover needs a high-contrast central element to read at IG-screenshot distance. The pupil's solid black against the cream paper is the most legible feature. |
| **Fiber-optic filaments at the edges** | Eyebeam's post-loft Bushwick studio aesthetic: 199 Cook Street, Suite 104, Brooklyn (since 2017). The studio's exposed-cable, networked-workstations character. Photos: [eyebeam.org](https://eyebeam.org), [Hyperallergic 2018 profile](https://hyperallergic.com/452528/eyebeam-bushwick-residency-program/). |
| **Faded periphery into cream paper** | Vignette; deliberately omits Eyebeam's two prior locations — 540 W 21st (Chelsea, 1998–2014) and Industry City (2014–2017) — and the unbuilt Diller Scofidio facade for the Chelsea expansion. |

## Founder + key people referenced

The cover's eye is generic — no portrait — but the form references the org's six-ED chain:
- **John S. Johnson III** — founder, 1997–2004. Funded the org from his own wealth.
- **Steven Tremble** — interim 2004–2005.
- **Amanda McDonald Crowley** — 2005–2011.
- **Patricia C. Jones** — 2011–2015.
- **Roddy Schrock** — 2015–2025 (the longest-serving). Pivoted to Bushwick.
- **Sheetal Prajapati** — interim, July 2025.
- **Lisa Kim Kaganskiy** (NEW INC founder) — incoming ED, April 2026. *Cross-references the NEW INC dossier.*

## What was deliberately excluded

- The unbuilt **Diller Scofidio + Renfro** Chelsea facade (1999–2002 design, never built; Eyebeam vacated 540 W 21st in 2014).
- The Industry City interim space (2014–2017).
- The 1998 founding-press portraits of John S. Johnson III.
- The "ribbon-architecture" curving facade from the original prompt iteration.
