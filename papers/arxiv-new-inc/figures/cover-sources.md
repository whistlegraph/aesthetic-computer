# Cover-prompt sources — NEW INC

> The current cover is a placeholder cream-paper PNG (OpenAI billing limit hit before regen). When billing is raised, run `node papers/bin/gen-cover.mjs arxiv-new-inc --force` and the prompt below will produce the canonical cover.

## Visual elements → sources

| Element | Source |
|---|---|
| **Hexagonal incubator pod / honeycomb cell** | The **incubator** metaphor made literal. NEW INC was founded in 2014 as the New Museum's "cultural incubator" — the first museum-led incubator program. The hexagonal-cell form reads instantly as "incubator pod" while staying abstract enough to be a logo-mark. Hexagonal-honeycomb is also a [recurring NEW INC visual motif](https://www.newinc.org) on their own site. |
| **Translucent walls, faintly luminous** | The cohort-centric NEW INC posture: members work *inside* the museum (231 Bowery), and the relationship is intentionally semi-transparent — neither fully embedded staff nor fully outside tenants. |
| **Soldering iron + paintbrush + folded code-tape inside the pod** | The five current tracks (Year 12, 2025–26): **Art & Code** (with Rhizome), **Creative Science** (Simons), **Extended Realities** (EY + Onassis ONX), **Social Architecture** (Lewis Foundation), **Cooperative Studies**. The three tools collapse the multi-disciplinary track lineup into one image: soldering-iron = hardware, paintbrush = visual art, code-tape = software. |
| **Single small figure inside the pod** | A nod to the **~74 new members per year** who pass through NEW INC. Generic, no specific likeness. |
| **Faded periphery** | Vignette; deliberately omits the 231 Bowery building exterior (which belongs visually to the New Museum proper, not NEW INC). |

## Founder + key people referenced

- **Lisa Phillips** — New Museum Director (1999–April 2026); co-founded NEW INC in 2014. *Retiring April 2026.*
- **Karen Wong** — co-founded NEW INC, 2014.
- **Lisa Kim Kaganskiy** (sometimes "Julia Kaganskiy") — founding director, 2014–April 2018. Now ED at Eyebeam (April 2026). *Cross-references the Eyebeam dossier.*
- **Stephanie Pereira** — director July 2018–May 2021 (ex-Kickstarter).
- **Salome Asega** — director July 2021–. Ex-Ford Foundation, prior NEW INC member 2016–17. [Cultured Magazine 2024 profile](https://www.culturedmag.com/article/2024/02/13/salome-asega-new-inc-young-artists-2024).

## What was deliberately excluded

- **Any portrait of Phillips, Wong, Kaganskiy, Pereira, or Asega** — leadership transitions are rapid.
- **The New Museum's iconic boxy-stack facade** (SANAA, 2007) — that's the parent org's visual identity, not NEW INC's.
- The 2026 OMA $82M expansion that gives NEW INC its first dedicated permanent space — too forward-looking; the building isn't built.
- Any specific cohort member's project.
