A single worn library card-catalog index card, shown face-on and slightly tilted, the classic ruled buff manila reference card with a round punch-hole at its bottom edge where a brass catalog rod would thread through. The ruled lines are hand-drawn in faded blue, and on the card a short handwritten bibliographic citation sits in soft graphite pencil — author, year, title — kept deliberately illegible, just the rhythm of a reference entry. One corner of the card is dog-eared and the manila is faintly age-foxed, an object pulled from a long drawer of cited names. It is the lone subject, floating on bare cream paper.
