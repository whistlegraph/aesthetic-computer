Bonneville Psychiatry

Considered psychiatric care, by appointment.

A clinic dedicated to women's-focused and general psychiatric care.

The Practice

Care is provided by a triple board-certified nurse practitioner in midwifery, family practice, and psychiatry (CNM, FNP, PMHNP), with a Doctor of Nursing Practice from the University of Utah, 2016.

The work is unhurried and individual. A small, selective practice — time per patient over volume — delivered entirely by telehealth.

Reproductive Psychiatry

“I specialize in reproductive psychiatry — helping individuals navigate the unique challenges that arise during menstruation, pregnancy, postpartum, menopause, and beyond.”
