Keeps Wallet
A Tezos wallet for your keeps collection.
Import your seed phrase from Temple or Kukai.
Download Keeps Wallet
Install
1 Unzip the downloaded file
2 Open chrome://extensions in Chrome
3 Enable Developer mode (top right toggle)
4 Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder
5 Click the extension icon, import your seed phrase, and see your keeps
Your keeps, alive
Open the wallet and see your collection. That's the whole UI.
Beacon compatible
Works with any Tezos dApp — keep.kidlisp.com, objkt.com, and more.
Self-custody
Your keys never leave the extension. Encrypted with your password.
Import from Temple
Same seed phrase, same address. Your keeps come with you.
