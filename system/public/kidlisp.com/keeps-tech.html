How It Works

Each keep is an FA2 token on Tezos. Your KidLisp source is bundled into a self-contained HTML file, a thumbnail is rendered, and both are pinned to IPFS. No KidLisp servers are needed to view your token — it works on objkt and any FA2 marketplace.

Duplicate keeps are rejected on-chain by content hash. Royalties (9% author, 1% platform) are encoded at keep time and honored by all FA2 marketplaces.

Your Guarantees

  • Only you can keep. A short-lived signed permit ties the transaction to your wallet and content hash. No admin mint path exists.
  • Only you can burn. Admin cannot burn on your behalf.
  • Content and royalties are immutable. Nobody can change what you made or what you earn.
  • You can freeze metadata. Irreversible, owner-only — not even admin can undo it.
  • Transfers always work. Even if the contract is paused, FA2 transfers stay live. You can always move or sell.
