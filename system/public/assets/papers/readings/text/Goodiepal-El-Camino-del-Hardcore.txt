to be appreciated by human beings but also to be appreciated by a possible future
artificial, or as I prefer to call it, alternative intelligence!

It is a piece that challenges all my previous ideas about music and can as such be}
Seen as highly speculative. Nevertheless, I feel that I can stand up for the
qualities it holds and is therefore ready to bring it into a broader circulation.

My English is rather appalling and so is every other language or tongue that I try to
