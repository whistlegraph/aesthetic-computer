Support NCC 

▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒

NELA Computer Club is member-supported and decentralized — no owner, no host, no sponsor steering the ship. We keep the lights on together.

Showing up is always free. There's no prerequisite, no paywall, no cover charge to learn alongside us every other Tuesday. But a real room costs real money, and your contribution keeps the door open to everyone.

Where it goes

▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒

Every dollar is spent in the open and accounted for by the board:

  • Space at Plot in Chinatown.
  • Coffee, snacks, and the occasional cable.
  • Servers, domains, and the internal tools that keep NCC running.
  • A scholarship fund so cost is never a reason to stay home.
