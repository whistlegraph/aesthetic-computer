Jeffrey Alan Scudder

Painter · b. 1989, Assonet, MA

Bio

Jeffrey Alan Scudder (b. 1989, Assonet, MA) is an American painter and artist-programmer. He works across stretched canvas, digital painting, and performance. Scudder is the creator of Aesthetic Computer and Whistlegraph, and the author of A Manifesto for Radical Digital Painting. He has presented over 65 lecture-performances across the US and Europe.

His work is in the collections of KADIST (San Francisco) and SMK (National Gallery of Denmark). He received the Hash Award at ZKM Karlsruhe in 2018. He holds an MFA from Yale School of Art (2013) and a BFA from Ringling College of Art and Design (2011).
