A drawing you sing  ·  Est. 2019
A whistlegraph is a drawing you sing. Every line is a syllable. You draw it while you sing it, and the finished picture is a score — it teaches you how to play it. I started making them in 2019; the trio drew millions of them onto TikTok. This is the index.
Every whistlegraph has a code.
Type imab at aesthetic.computer to perform it — or open whistlegraph.org/imab. Four little letters, one whole song.

A rough spine

2019 The form is invented. “I'm a butterfly” — four strokes, four sung lines.
2020 The trio forms in a cabin in Ashland. TikTok tutorials: a hand draws while a voice sings.
2021 Rhizome commissions The Longest Whistlegraph Ever (So Far).
2022 Ten Whistlegraphs at Feral File. The New Museum. Taipei Dangdai.
2023 The zine (Sex Magazine, 750 copies). The trio parts. The form continues, solo.
Now whistlegraph.org — past, present, and future of the artform, in one place.
