Aesthetic Computer's Privacy Policy

Aesthetic Computer keeps finished paintings and artworks on a remote server so they can be shared with and viewed by users.

Aesthetic Computer allows you to associate an email with a @handle to link with your artwork and realtime game activity.

Aesthetic Computer will request permission to access your device's camera in order to take pictures in commands like camera and for playing with games that have handtracking, such as handtime.

Aesthetic Computer will request permission to access your device's microphone in order to record in games like baktok.

Aesthetic Computer connects to realtime multiplayer servers for games like pond and field.

Aesthetic Computer does not sell or exchange any user data with third parties.

Delete your Aesthetic Computer account by entering delete-erase-and-forget-me in the prompt.

For more information enter help to communicate with the author.
