Aesthetic Computer's Privacy Policy

Aesthetic Computer saves your finished paintings on a remote server so they can be reloaded via a generated code. Aesthetic Computer will request permission to access your device's photo library in order to save the paintings that you create to your device. Aesthetic Computer will request permission to access your device's camera in order to take pictures in commands like "camera" and for playing with games that have handtracking, such as "handtime". Aesthetic Computer will request permission to access your device's microphone in order to record in games like "baktok." Aesthetic Computer does not collect or store any other information.
