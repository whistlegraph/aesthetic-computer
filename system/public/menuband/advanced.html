Advanced

Download Menu Band directly for a few extra Mac features.

Most people want the Mac App Store version. It updates automatically and plays the same instruments. Choose this download if you want the extra features below.

What this version can do

  • Type to play in any app. Keep playing notes while you work in another window.
  • Open with ⌘⌘. Double-tap Command to bring up Menu Band from anywhere.
  • Play with nearby Macs. Menu Band finds them and connects automatically.
  • Play with more than one finger. Turn your trackpad into an expressive musical surface.

Good to know

  • Updates come directly from Menu Band. The app will let you know when a new version is ready.
  • Typing in other apps needs permission. Turn on Menu Band in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility.
  • The first launch may take a moment. macOS checks the app before opening it.
