Self-Hosted ATProto Personal Data Server

This is the federated data layer for Aesthetic Computer — a mobile-first runtime and social network for creative computing.

Every painting, mood, tape, piece of code, and paper created on AC is stored here as an open ATProto record, addressable by anyone on the network. Your data lives on infrastructure we operate, not on a platform you don't control.

Learn about operating costs at bills.aesthetic.computer. Help fund infrastructure at give.aesthetic.computer to keep the network free and open source.
